# RFC-0004: Launch Mode

## Summary

This proposal recommends making launch decisions explicit and evidence-based.

## Motivation

Launch readiness should not rely on assumptions or informal confidence.

## Proposal

- use GO, GO WITH RISKS, or NO-GO as explicit launch outcomes
- require a clear review of security, rollout, and rollback readiness
- document known risks before release

## Consequences

This improves launch discipline and operational clarity.
