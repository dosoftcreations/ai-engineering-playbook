# Incident Playbook

## Incident priorities

1. Protect users.
2. Protect data.
3. Restore service.
4. Preserve evidence.
5. Communicate clearly.
6. Prevent recurrence.

## Incident workflow

```text
Detect
↓
Triage severity
↓
Mitigate
↓
Communicate
↓
Resolve
↓
Review
```

## AI agent rules during incident

- Do not refactor.
- Do not speculate.
- Use evidence.
- Keep changes minimal.
- Preserve logs.
- State uncertainty.
- Ask before destructive actions.

## Post-incident review

Include:

- Timeline
- Impact
- Root cause
- What worked
- What failed
- Preventive actions
- Owner
- Due date
