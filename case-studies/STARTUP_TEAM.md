# Case Study: Startup Team

This case study describes how a startup team can use AI-assisted workflows to move quickly while preserving review discipline.

## Context

The team needed to ship quickly but still maintain a reasonable level of quality and accountability.

## Approach

- use short planning cycles and clear scope boundaries
- keep release and rollback practices explicit
- rely on lightweight checklists for pre-release and production readiness
- use human review for security-sensitive changes

## Outcome

The team improved delivery speed without abandoning the basic controls needed for safe growth.
