# Case Study: AI-First Company

This case study describes how an AI-first company can keep engineering quality high while moving quickly.

## Context

The company used AI throughout the development lifecycle and needed a disciplined approach that would not degrade quality.

## Approach

- use clear standards for review, security, and release readiness
- keep model selection aligned with task complexity
- preserve explicit human ownership for critical decisions
- track outcomes and rework rather than relying on hype

## Outcome

The workflow remained effective because the company treated AI as an engineering aid rather than a shortcut for discipline.
