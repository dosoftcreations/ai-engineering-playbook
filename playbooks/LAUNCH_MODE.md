# Launch Mode Playbook

Use this playbook for production launches and release candidates.

## Launch checklist

- Confirm the scope and intended rollout.
- Verify authentication, authorization, and critical flows.
- Review security-sensitive changes.
- Confirm monitoring, alerts, and rollback readiness.
- Prepare a smoke test and a rollback plan.

## Launch decision

Use one of the following outcomes:

- GO
- GO WITH RISKS
- NO-GO

A NO-GO decision should be made when a blocker affects correctness, security, or safe rollout.
