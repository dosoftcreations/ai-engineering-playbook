# Incident Response Playbook

Use this playbook when a production issue requires immediate coordination.

## Immediate actions

1. Contain the impact and reduce further damage.
2. Preserve evidence for diagnosis.
3. Assign clear ownership for mitigation and communication.
4. Decide whether the issue requires rollback, disabling a feature, or a temporary mitigation.

## Follow-up

- Document the root cause and the mitigation taken.
- Record what changed and why.
- Identify gaps in prevention, monitoring, or review.
